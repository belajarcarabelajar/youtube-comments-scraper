# Support

## Maintenance Expectations
This project is maintained by community volunteers. We aim to respond to issues and pull requests within 1-2 weeks. Please be patient and respectful of our time.

## Support Channels
- For bugs and feature requests, please use the [GitHub Issue Tracker](../../issues).
- For general questions and discussions, feel free to open a Discussion on GitHub.

We do not provide private support via email or direct messages.
